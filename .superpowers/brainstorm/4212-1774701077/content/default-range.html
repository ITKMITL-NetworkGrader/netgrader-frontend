What should the default date range be when the tab first opens?

This is what the instructor sees before touching any filter

A

Full lab window (availableFrom → availableUntil)

Shows everything from when the lab opened to when it closes. For a 2-week lab this gives you the complete picture including overtime.

Jan 19 09:30 → Feb 02 24:00 (14 days → daily buckets)
✓ Always complete picture on first load
± Wide range means daily buckets — less hourly detail by default
B

Exam window only (availableFrom → dueDate)

Defaults to the "official" exam period. Overtime submissions are hidden until the instructor zooms out. Matches what most instructors care about first.

Jan 19 09:30 → Jan 20 20:10 (2 days → hourly buckets)
✓ Exam day shows hourly detail immediately
± Overtime students invisible until range is widened
C

Last 7 days (rolling window)

Always shows the most recent 7 days regardless of lab dates. Useful for active labs, but confusing for past exams.

Mar 21 00:00 → Mar 28 23:59 (today-based)
✓ Always "current" for live labs
✗ Shows empty data for past exams
Also applies to all 4 charts: KPI cards, Execution Time, Pass Rate, and Timeline will all filter by the selected date range — so every number on the page reflects the same window.
